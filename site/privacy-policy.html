General information

Welcome to our Privacy Policy page! At Sejuta Ranting, your trust matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. We collect data to improve your browsing experience and provide tailored recommendations, such as Bonsai care tips and related services. By using our services, you consent to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please take a moment to read through this document and understand your rights.

Right to access, correct and delete data and to object to data processing

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time by reaching out to us via our contact form. At Sejuta Ranting, we take appropriate steps to safeguard your information from unauthorized access or misuse. Please note, however, that we may include links to third-party websites on our platform. While we carefully curate these resources, we cannot be held responsible for their content or data policies.

Management of personal data

We respect your preferences when it comes to your personal data. You can choose to receive updates about our Bonsai-related services via email or opt out anytime by adjusting your communication preferences in your account settings. We also ensure that your contact details are not shared with third parties without your consent. If you have any concerns regarding data collection or use, feel free to contact our support team.

Information We Collect

Sejuta Ranting collects data to deliver the best user experience while providing personalized Bonsai-related services. Some data is provided directly, such as when you create an account or make an inquiry. Other information is gathered through your interactions on our website, including the use of cookies, analytics, and third-party services. For example, we may use location-based data to recommend Bonsai care tips suited to your environment. All data collection is aimed at enhancing your journey with us.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide personalized Bonsai care recommendations, enhance communication by keeping you informed about updates and special offers, and optimize our website's functionality to ensure a seamless browsing experience. Your privacy and data security remain our top priorities as we strive to support your Bonsai journey.

Sharing Your Information

We only share your personal data with your consent or to fulfill specific requests, such as completing an order. For example, when you make a purchase, your payment details may be processed through secure third-party financial services. We may also work with trusted partners to improve our website and services, such as hosting providers or analytics tools, ensuring they adhere to strict privacy standards. Your data will never be used for purposes outside of what has been outlined in this policy.
